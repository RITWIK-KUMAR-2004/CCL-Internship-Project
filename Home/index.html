About Scheme

Central Coalfields Limited (CCL) under its CSR initiative has been providing free of cost coaching along with

boarding and lodging facility to the meritorious students since 2012 under its CSR PROJECT- CCL KE LAL LAADLI.

The selected students are groomed for competitive engineering entrance examinations for getting into IITs

and prestigious engineering colleges. Students are taught by Officers who are IIT Alumni. The scheme is running

successfully since 2012 and has given tremendous results.
